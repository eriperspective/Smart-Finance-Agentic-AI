SMARTFINANCE AI - FRAUD PREVENTION AND SECURITY POLICIES

FRAUD DETECTION SYSTEM

Our advanced AI-powered fraud detection system monitors all transactions 24/7 for suspicious activity. We analyze hundreds of data points including:
- Transaction location and merchant
- Purchase patterns and spending behavior
- Device and IP address information
- Transaction velocity and frequency
- Historical account activity

TRANSACTION MONITORING

Real-Time Alerts:
- Unusual spending patterns detected
- Large transactions exceeding typical amounts
- International transactions (if not common)
- Multiple transactions in short timeframe
- Transactions from new devices or locations
- High-risk merchant categories

Automatic Holds:
Transactions may be automatically blocked if:
- Amount exceeds $5,000 (standard accounts)
- Multiple failed PIN attempts
- Transaction from high-risk country
- Unusual location (far from your typical locations)
- First international transaction
- Merchant flagged for fraud

CUSTOMER RESPONSIBILITIES

Protect Your Information:
- Never share password or PIN with anyone
- Don't write down passwords in accessible places
- Use unique passwords (not used on other sites)
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Keep contact information current

Monitor Your Account:
- Review transactions daily
- Set up transaction alerts
- Check monthly statements
- Report discrepancies within 60 days
- Verify mobile deposit success

Report Unauthorized Activity:
- Lock your card immediately in the app
- Call fraud hotline: 1-800-FRAUD-01 (24/7)
- Submit fraud report through app or website
- File police report for identity theft
- Request new card and account numbers

Secure Your Devices:
- Keep app updated to latest version
- Use device passcode or biometric lock
- Don't jailbreak or root your device
- Install security updates promptly
- Use secure WiFi (avoid public networks for banking)
- Enable remote wipe capability

FRAUD PREVENTION BEST PRACTICES

Safe Online Shopping:
- Verify website security (HTTPS, lock icon)
- Shop with reputable merchants
- Use credit card over debit for better protection
- Check merchant reviews before purchase
- Save receipts and confirmation emails
- Enable virtual card numbers for one-time use

ATM Safety:
- Use ATMs in well-lit, monitored locations
- Cover PIN pad when entering code
- Inspect ATM for skimming devices
- Take receipt or ensure it prints nothing
- Don't accept help from strangers
- If card is retained, call bank immediately

Phishing Protection:
Red flags for phishing:
- Urgent requests for account information
- Suspicious sender email addresses
- Spelling and grammar errors
- Requests to click unknown links
- Threats of account closure
- Requests for password or PIN

Remember: We will NEVER:
- Ask for your full password
- Request PIN via email or phone
- Send unsolicited links to click
- Threaten immediate account closure
- Request remote access to your device

IDENTITY THEFT PROTECTION

Warning Signs:
- Unexpected credit card or account statements
- Denial of credit for unknown reasons
- Calls about purchases you didn't make
- Missing mail or redirected mail
- Accounts you didn't open appearing on credit report

If You're a Victim:
1. Lock all cards and accounts immediately
2. File police report
3. Place fraud alert with credit bureaus
4. Review credit reports from all three bureaus
5. Contact Social Security Administration (SSN theft)
6. File FTC identity theft report
7. Change all passwords and PINs
8. Monitor accounts closely for 12+ months

We Provide:
- Free credit monitoring for fraud victims
- Identity theft resolution assistance
- Zero liability for unauthorized transactions
- New account numbers and cards
- Ongoing monitoring and alerts

ACCOUNT TAKEOVER PREVENTION

High-Risk Indicators:
- Login from unusual location
- Multiple failed login attempts
- Request to change email/phone
- Request for large transfers shortly after login
- Disabling of security features
- Adding new external accounts

Protection Measures:
- Login notifications to email/phone
- Verification required for profile changes
- Cooling-off period for new external accounts (3 days)
- Transfer limits on newly added accounts
- Multi-factor authentication enforcement
- Session timeout after 15 minutes inactivity

MERCHANT FRAUD

Chargeback Rights:
- Dispute unauthorized charges within 60 days
- Temporary credit during investigation
- We investigate all disputes within 10 business days
- Keep documentation (receipts, communication)
- Deadline: 60 days from statement date

Common Merchant Fraud:
- Charging more than authorized amount
- Charging for undelivered goods/services
- Subscription charges after cancellation
- Fake charities
- Advance-fee scams

CRYPTOCURRENCY AND INVESTMENT SCAMS

Warning Signs:
- Guaranteed high returns with no risk
- Pressure to invest immediately
- Unsolicited investment advice
- Too-good-to-be-true opportunities
- Request for upfront fees
- Pyramid or multi-level marketing schemes

Remember:
- Cryptocurrency transactions are irreversible
- We don't offer cryptocurrency insurance
- Verify investment advisors credentials
- Research before investing
- Report suspicious investment offers

COMPROMISED CARD PROCEDURES

If Card is Lost or Stolen:
1. Lock card immediately in app (Settings > Cards > Lock)
2. Review recent transactions for fraud
3. Report as lost/stolen in app or call support
4. Request replacement card:
   - Standard delivery: 7-10 business days (free)
   - Express delivery: 2-3 business days ($25 fee)
5. Update recurring payments with new card number

Temporary Card Numbers:
Generate virtual card numbers for:
- Online shopping
- Subscription trials
- Untrusted merchants
- One-time purchases
Settings > Cards > Generate Virtual Card
