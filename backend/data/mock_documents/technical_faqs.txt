SMARTFINANCE AI - TECHNICAL SUPPORT FAQS AND TROUBLESHOOTING

LOGIN ISSUES

Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Enter your email address and we'll send a reset link. The link expires in 30 minutes. Create a password with at least 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.

Q: My account is locked. What should I do?
A: Accounts lock automatically after 5 failed login attempts. Wait 30 minutes for automatic unlock, or click "Unlock Account" to verify your identity via email or SMS code.

Q: I'm not receiving the verification code.
A: Check your spam folder. Codes expire after 10 minutes. Ensure your phone number is correct in Settings > Profile. Try requesting a new code. If issues persist, contact support for manual verification.

Q: Two-factor authentication isn't working.
A: Ensure your device clock is synced correctly. Try regenerating authentication codes in Settings > Security > 2FA. Keep backup codes in a safe place. You can temporarily disable 2FA by verifying your identity via email.

MOBILE APP ISSUES

Q: The app keeps crashing on startup.
A: Try these steps in order:
1. Force close the app completely
2. Clear app cache (Settings > Apps > SmartFinance > Clear Cache)
3. Ensure you have the latest version from app store
4. Restart your device
5. Uninstall and reinstall the app
Your data is stored securely and will sync after reinstallation.

Q: Transactions aren't showing up in real-time.
A: Pull down on the transaction screen to refresh. Check your internet connection. Some merchant transactions take 1-3 business days to post. Pending transactions show in a separate section. Clear cache if transactions are missing after 24 hours.

Q: The app is running slowly.
A: Performance tips:
- Close other apps running in background
- Clear app cache weekly
- Ensure OS is up to date
- Minimum requirements: iOS 14+ or Android 10+
- Free up device storage (app needs 100MB minimum)
- Disable animations in Settings > Accessibility > Reduce Motion

Q: Face ID or fingerprint login stopped working.
A: Re-enable biometric login:
1. Go to Settings > Security > Biometric Login
2. Toggle off, then on
3. Re-authenticate with password
4. Re-scan fingerprint or face
Ensure your device biometrics are set up in phone settings.

ACCOUNT ACCESS ISSUES

Q: I can't see my account balance.
A: This usually indicates a sync issue:
1. Pull to refresh the screen
2. Log out and log back in
3. Check if you're connected to internet
4. Clear app cache
5. Update to latest app version
Balance should appear within 60 seconds.

Q: My recent deposit isn't showing.
A: Check deposit timeline:
- Mobile check deposit: Available in 1-2 business days
- Direct deposit: Available same day if before 8 PM EST
- Wire transfer: Within 1 business day
- ACH transfer: 2-3 business days
Check "Pending Deposits" section for status.

Q: I'm seeing an "Account Restricted" message.
A: Account restrictions occur for:
- Suspicious activity detected (fraud prevention)
- Incomplete KYC verification
- Outstanding negative balance
- Terms of service violation
Contact support immediately with your account number for resolution.

TRANSFER AND PAYMENT ISSUES

Q: My transfer failed. Why?
A: Common reasons:
- Insufficient funds
- Daily transfer limit exceeded ($5,000 for standard accounts)
- Recipient account information incorrect
- Network timeout (try again)
- Account on hold for security review
Check transaction history for specific error code.

Q: How do I increase my transfer limit?
A: Daily limits:
- Standard: $5,000
- Premium: $25,000
- Business: $100,000
Request temporary increase: Settings > Account > Request Limit Increase
Permanent increase requires account review (5-7 business days).

Q: Bill payment didn't process.
A: Verify:
- Payee information is correct
- Account has sufficient funds
- Payment scheduled for future date hasn't arrived yet
- Payment method is active (not expired)
Bill payments process next business day if scheduled before 5 PM EST.

MOBILE DEPOSIT ISSUES

Q: Check deposit was rejected. Why?
A: Rejection reasons:
- Check image too blurry (retake in good lighting)
- Check amount exceeds daily limit ($3,000)
- Check already deposited (duplicate)
- Check is more than 6 months old
- Signature missing or doesn't match
Write "VOID" on rejected checks to prevent duplicate deposits.

Q: How do I take a clear check photo?
A: Best practices:
- Use plain dark background
- Ensure all four corners visible
- Avoid shadows and glare
- Hold phone directly above check
- Keep check flat and unwrinkled
- Good lighting (natural light best)

SECURITY AND PRIVACY

Q: I see transactions I didn't make.
A: Immediate action required:
1. Lock your card immediately (Settings > Cards > Lock Card)
2. Report fraud in app: Support > Report Fraud
3. Review all recent transactions
4. Change your password
5. Enable 2FA if not active
We have zero liability for reported unauthorized transactions.

Q: How do I enable security alerts?
A: Configure alerts:
Settings > Notifications > Transaction Alerts
Options:
- All transactions
- Transactions over $X amount
- International transactions
- Online purchases
- ATM withdrawals
Receive via push notification, email, or SMS.

Q: Someone has my login information. What do I do?
A: Emergency security steps:
1. Change password immediately
2. Log out all devices (Settings > Security > Active Sessions > Log Out All)
3. Enable 2FA
4. Review recent account activity
5. Lock all cards temporarily
6. Contact support for security review

COMMON ERROR CODES

Error 1001: Network connection lost - Check internet and retry
Error 1002: Session expired - Log in again
Error 1003: Invalid credentials - Reset password
Error 2001: Insufficient funds - Add funds to account
Error 2002: Daily limit exceeded - Wait 24 hours or request increase
Error 3001: Temporary account hold - Contact support
Error 3002: Card declined - Check with merchant or contact support
Error 4001: Server maintenance - Try again in 15 minutes
Error 5001: Device not recognized - Verify via email

CONNECTIVITY ISSUES

Q: App says "Unable to connect to server."
A: Troubleshooting:
1. Check internet connection (WiFi or mobile data)
2. Try switching between WiFi and mobile data
3. Disable VPN temporarily
4. Check our status page: status.smartfinanceai.com
5. Wait 5 minutes and retry
If server maintenance, you'll see notification in app.

Q: Can I use the app on multiple devices?
A: Yes, log in on up to 5 devices simultaneously. Each device requires one-time verification. Manage active devices: Settings > Security > Active Devices. Remove old devices to free up slots.
